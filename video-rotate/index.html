Rotate Video Online

Did you accidentally record in the wrong orientation? Fix sideways video instantly with our secure browser tool. Whether you need to rotate video 90 degrees or flip video horizontally, you can do it locally on your device in seconds for free.

How to Rotate or Flip Video Online

  1. Select your video: Drop your upside-down or sideways footage into our editor.
  2. Choose your rotation: Click to rotate video 90 degrees clockwise/counter-clockwise, or apply a horizontal or vertical flip.
  3. Apply and save: Export your corrected video back to your device instantly.

Why Use Our Secure Video Rotator?

You shouldn't have to upload your personal moments to a remote server just to fix sideways video. Our platform uses WebAssembly to process your media entirely on your own device. This ensures you can rotate video online with zero upload delays and 100% privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I flip video horizontally like a mirror?

Yes! Our tool includes a mirror function that lets you flip video horizontally, which is perfect for correcting selfie-camera footage.

How do I rotate video 90 degrees?

Simply upload your clip and click the left or right rotation arrow. One click will perfectly rotate video 90 degrees.

Does this tool support a vertical flip?

Yes, along with rotation and horizontal mirroring, you can apply a vertical flip to turn your footage upside down entirely for creative effects.
