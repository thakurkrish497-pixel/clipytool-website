Why Use Our Browser-Based Lossless Video Merger?

Uploading multiple large video files to standard online mergers can take hours. Clipy Studio processes your files locally using WebAssembly. This allows you to combine multiple videos instantly, ensuring a secure, private, and blazing-fast workflow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I combine multiple videos of different formats?

Yes! Our engine can take various formats like MP4, MOV, and WebM and seamlessly join video clips together into a single unified file.

Is this a true lossless video merger?

If your input clips share the exact same codec and resolution, our tool can often merge videos online without re-encoding, preserving 100% of the original quality.

Is there a file size limit to merge videos online?

No. Because the merging happens on your local machine, you are only limited by your own computer's memory, completely bypassing cloud server restrictions.

All processing happens locally. Your videos never leave your device.
