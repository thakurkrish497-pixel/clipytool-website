Crop Video Online (Free & Secure)

Need to center crop a video for social media? Our free browser cropper lets you easily change video aspect ratios from 16:9 to 9:16 in seconds. Because everything runs locally in your browser, your files remain 100% private and are never uploaded to any servers.

How to Crop Video Online

  1. Select your file: Drag and drop your video directly into the browser.
  2. Choose the aspect ratio: Select a preset (like 9:16 for Reels/TikTok) or adjust the frame to center crop your video perfectly.
  3. Export instantly: Click export to process your video locally at blazing-fast speeds.

Why Use Our Free Browser Cropper?

Unlike traditional video editors that force you to wait for slow server uploads, Clipy Studio uses advanced WebAssembly (WASM) technology. This means you can crop video online instantly using your own device's processing power. There are no file size limits, no hidden fees, and absolute privacy guaranteed.
