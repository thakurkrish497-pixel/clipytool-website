Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a video to GIF?

Simply upload your video file (MP4, WebM, MOV, etc.), set your desired start and end times to trim the video, select your preferred resolution and framerate, and click "Convert to GIF".

Are my videos uploaded to a server?

No! Clipy Studio is completely private. All conversions run entirely inside your browser using WebAssembly. Your files never leave your device.
